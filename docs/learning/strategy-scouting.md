## What this role does

Strategy and scouting members turn the game and the data into decisions: what
robot to build, how to play matches, and which alliances to pick.

## Learning path

1. **Read the game** — scoring, penalties, and the highest-value actions.
2. **Define team strategy** — your role in a match and robot priorities.
3. **Build a scouting system** — what data to collect and how to record it.
4. **Analyze data** — turning match data into rankings and pick lists.
5. **Match & alliance strategy** — pre-match plans and alliance selection.

## Suggested training project

Design a scouting sheet for the current game, scout several matches (live or from
video), and produce a simple ranked pick list with your reasoning.
