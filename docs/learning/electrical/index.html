Electrical, Sensors & Pneumatics Curriculum

A role-based path for the electrical team. Work through the lessons in order: each one builds on the last, from working safely around a battery to diagnosing fault codes on a fully wired robot — including sensors and a complete pneumatics system.

Roles

Electrical technicians grow through three roles. Each role is earned by completing the lessons labeled for it (and everything before it) and demonstrating the skills on a real robot.
