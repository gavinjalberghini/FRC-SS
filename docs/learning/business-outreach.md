## What this role does

Business and outreach members keep the team funded, organized, and connected to
the community — and tell the team's story to sponsors and judges.

## Learning path

1. **Team finances** — building and tracking a season budget.
2. **Sponsorship** — prospecting, outreach letters, and sponsor stewardship.
3. **Awards** — understanding the [FRC awards](https://www.firstinspires.org/resource-library/frc/awards) and the Impact Award.
4. **Outreach & impact** — planning events and measuring community reach.
5. **Storytelling** — presentations, the Impact essay, and judge interviews.

## Suggested training project

Draft a one-page sponsorship pitch and a simple season budget, then plan one
outreach event with goals and a way to measure its impact.
