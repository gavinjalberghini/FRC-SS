CAD & Design Curriculum

A role-based path that takes you from zero to modeling a full robot in Onshape. Work through the lessons in order — each one builds on the last, from your first box tube to a competition-ready mechanism. Adapted from the excellent FRCDesign.org Learning Course.

Roles

Designers grow through three roles. Each role is earned by completing the lessons labeled for it (and everything before it) and demonstrating the skills in CAD.
