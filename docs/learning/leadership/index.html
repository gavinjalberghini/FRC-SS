Leadership Curriculum

A general path for any member growing into a leadership role — on a technical subsystem or a non-technical one. Work through the lessons in order: each one builds on the last, from leading yourself to leading a small team to developing the leaders who come after you.

Roles

Leaders grow through three roles. Each role is earned by completing the lessons labeled for it (and everything before it) and demonstrating the skills with a real team.
