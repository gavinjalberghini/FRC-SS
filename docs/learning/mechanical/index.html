Mechanical & Fabrication Curriculum

A role-based, hands-on path for the mechanical team. Work through the lessons in order: each one builds on the last, from earning your shop safety sign-off to cutting and drilling real parts with power tools and assembling them into working mechanisms.

Roles

Mechanical members grow through three roles. Each role is earned by completing the lessons labeled for it (and everything before it) and demonstrating the skills safely in the shop.
