## What this role does

The drive team operates the robot during matches and keeps it running at events.
Roles include **driver**, **operator**, **human player**, **drive coach**, and
**technician**.

## Learning path

1. **Know the roles** — who does what during a 2.5-minute match.
2. **Practice, practice, practice** — log real driving hours before events.
3. **Match procedure** — pre-match checklist, comms, and field setup.
4. **In-match communication** — clear, calm callouts between driver and operator.
5. **Pit & repairs** — fast diagnosis and fixes between matches.

Write your team's pre-match checklist and run a timed drive practice on a mock
field, including a simulated mid-match repair in the pit.
