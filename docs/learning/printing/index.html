3D Printing Curriculum

A role-based path for 3D printing on the team, built around our Bambu Lab printers and Bambu Studio. Work through the lessons in order — from your first print to producing strong, competition-legal parts in engineering materials.

Roles

Print operators grow through three roles. Each role is earned by completing the lessons labeled for it (and everything before it) and demonstrating the skills on a printer.

Respect the heat and the fumes. Nozzles and beds run hot enough to burn, and some materials need an enclosure and ventilation. Never reach into a running printer, and follow the safety rules in Lesson 1.

📏

Printed parts must be competition-legal. The FRC game manual restricts where and how 3D-printed parts can be used each season. When this curriculum and the manual disagree, the manual wins.
