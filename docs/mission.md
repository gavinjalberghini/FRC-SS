## Mission Statement

> We build competitive robots and confident people. Through FIRST Robotics
> Competition, our team gives every member the chance to learn real engineering,
> business, and leadership skills in a culture of curiosity, respect, and
> Gracious Professionalism®.

## Vision

In three to five years, we want to be a team that:

- Competes consistently and qualifies for championship-level events.
- Is **student-led**, with mentors coaching rather than directing.
- Sustains a strong pipeline of new members year over year.
- Gives back to our community and to rookie teams in our region.

## Core Values

| Value | What it means in practice |
| --- | --- |
| **Gracious Professionalism®** | We compete fiercely and treat everyone — teammates and opponents — with respect. |
| **Safety first** | No deadline is worth an injury. We follow shop rules, every time. |
| **Learn by doing** | Members own real work. Mistakes are expected and are how we learn. |
| **Everyone contributes** | Technical and non-technical roles are equally valued. |
| **Leave it better** | We document, clean up, and hand off a stronger team to next year. |

## Coopertition® & Outreach

Beyond the robot, we measure success by our impact:

- Mentoring and supporting rookie and nearby teams.
- Sharing designs, code, and lessons learned openly.
- Promoting STEM in our schools and local community.
