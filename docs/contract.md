## 1. Conduct & Respect

- I will treat teammates, mentors, volunteers, and competitors with respect and **Gracious Professionalism®**.
- I will not engage in bullying, harassment, discrimination, or harmful language of any kind.
- I will communicate honestly and resolve disagreements constructively.
- I will represent the team well at school, at events, and online.

## 2. Commitment & Attendance

- I will attend scheduled meetings and notify a lead in advance if I cannot.
- I will arrive ready to work and stay engaged during meetings.
- I understand that build season requires extra hours and will communicate conflicts early.
- I will keep my school grades and family responsibilities in good standing first.

## 3. Safety

- I will follow all shop, tool, and event safety rules **at all times**.
- I will wear required personal protective equipment (PPE) such as safety glasses.
- I will only use tools and machines I have been trained and authorized to use.
- I will report any injury, near-miss, or unsafe condition to a mentor immediately.

## 4. Equipment & Workspace

- I will treat tools, parts, and the workspace with care.
- I will clean up my area and return tools at the end of every meeting.
- I will report damaged or missing equipment instead of hiding it.

## 5. Technology & Online Conduct

- I will use team accounts, computers, and software responsibly.
- I will follow the school's and team's acceptable-use and social media policies.
- I will protect team and sponsor information and post online respectfully.

## 6. Academics & Eligibility

- I will maintain the academic eligibility required by my school to participate.
- I will ask for help early if school workload affects my participation.

## 7. Consequences

Members who do not meet these expectations may receive a conversation, a
documented warning, reduced responsibilities, or removal from the team, depending
on severity. Safety violations are treated most seriously.
